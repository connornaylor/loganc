A little about me...

Hi! I’m Logan, a 13-year-old actor and performer with a big passion for storytelling, whether it’s on stage, on camera, or behind a microphone. I’ve been performing since I learned to walk and love bringing characters to life—whether it’s through acting, singing, dancing, or voice work.

I’ve appeared in Shrek, Legally Blonde and loads of smaller shows and I’m always excited to take on new challenges and grow as a performer.
